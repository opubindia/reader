Our History is Important
Artist: Nidhin Shobhana
“Buddhist- Ambedkarite Missionaries brought me home to Babasaheb. And it is
through Babasaheb that I see and appreciate my history. In the face of perpetual ridicule
and humiliation, Babasaheb tells us 'Your history is important'. Through his writings,
he brings dignity to 'lower-caste' Christians. He underlines the 'spiritual merit' of our
historical choice in the most lucid way possible. He also lays down questions for our
future.” ~Nidhin Shobhana
