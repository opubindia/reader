avoided religion altogether. Buddhism, on the other hand, is inherently
anti-caste, and so he chose it. These were the reasons why Babasaheb
chose to convert to Buddhism.

The truth is Buddhism, the Dhamma, and it cannot be long hidden for
its time has come.
